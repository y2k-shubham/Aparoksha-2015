Creating and decoding secret messages has played a pivotal role throughout history and in many fictional novels, from the Caesar Cipher by Julius Caesar to the Enigma Machine in World War II to the various codes in Dan Brown's novel The Da Vinci Code to Arthur Conan Doyle's Sherlock Holmes. Platzen is an event based on a similar ground with an objective to examine your skills of number crunching and decoding ciphers. So pick up your thinking hats and start decoding!
